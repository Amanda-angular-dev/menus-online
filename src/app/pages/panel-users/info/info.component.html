How to Create Your Menu

You can create the data for your menu by clicking the “Create Data Menu” button.

Once it’s created, you can send that data to us, and an administrator will use it to build the menu.

You can also click the “Look Your Data List Menu” button to view and check the structure of the data you created before submitting it.

Regardless of whether you're creating the menu items or reviewing your data list, you must submit the menu to our administrators once you're finished so they can receive and process it properly.
